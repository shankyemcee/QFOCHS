Rate the quality of summaries for a chart

Given a chart, its title, its question and three answers, rate answers for factuality, relevance and fluency.

Definitions

Factual Correctness How accurate the summary is. A factually correct summary should only contain information (e.g. numbers, events, entities) that is true and/or supported by the chart.
Relevance How consistent the generated text is with respect to the query. A relevant answer should address the question and should only include information derivable from the chart.
Fluency How grammatically correct the summary is. A fluent summary should contain no grammatical errors and sound natural to a native English speaker.
